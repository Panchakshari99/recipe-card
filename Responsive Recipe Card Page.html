Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper.

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g pecorino cheese, grated
  • 50g parmesan, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt

Preparation:

Boil the pasta. In a bowl, beat the eggs and mix with cheese. Fry pancetta until crisp. Combine pasta with pancetta and remove from heat. Quickly mix in the egg and cheese mixture. Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper.

Chicken Tacos

Flavorful Mexican chicken tacos with fresh salsa and creamy avocado.

  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • 400g shredded cooked chicken
  • 1 cup salsa
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • Fresh cilantro
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Preparation:

Warm tortillas. Mix chicken with salsa. Assemble tacos by adding chicken mix, avocado slices, sour cream, and cilantro. Serve with lime wedges.

Classic Pancakes

Fluffy and golden pancakes perfect for a weekend breakfast.

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter

Preparation:

Combine dry ingredients. Add milk, beaten egg, and melted butter; mix until smooth. Heat a griddle or frying pan and pour batter to form pancakes. Cook until bubbles form, flip and cook until golden. Serve warm with syrup or fruit.
